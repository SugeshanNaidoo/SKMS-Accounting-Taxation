About SKMS

Your trusted financial partners since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018, SKMS has grown from a small accounting practice to a comprehensive financial services firm. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with our clients through exceptional service, integrity, and expertise.

Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience in accounting, taxation, and financial advisory services. We understand that every client is unique, which is why we take a personalized approach to every engagement.

From individual tax returns to complex business financial management, SKMS delivers reliable, accurate, and timely services that help our clients achieve their financial goals and maintain peace of mind.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional financial services that empower our clients to achieve their business and personal financial goals through expert guidance, innovative solutions, and unwavering integrity.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected accounting firm in KwaZulu-Natal, known for our commitment to excellence, client satisfaction, and positive impact on the communities we serve.
